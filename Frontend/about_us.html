About City Bus Finder

Our Mission

At City Bus Finder, our mission is to make public transportation more accessible, reliable, and convenient for everyone. We believe that efficient public transit is key to reducing congestion, lowering emissions, and creating more livable cities.

By providing accurate real-time information and intuitive route planning tools, we aim to increase ridership on public buses and contribute to more sustainable urban transportation networks.

Our Story

City Bus Finder was founded in 2022 by a group of urban transportation enthusiasts who were frustrated by the unpredictability of bus schedules and the difficulty of navigating complex transit systems in major cities.

2022

The Beginning

Founded by a team of transit enthusiasts and software developers who wanted to improve the public transportation experience.

2023

Initial Launch

Released our first app version in three major cities, with basic route planning and schedule information.

2024

Real-time Integration

Partnered with local transit authorities to integrate real-time GPS tracking data, significantly improving the accuracy of arrival predictions.

2025

Expansion

Now available in over 25 cities worldwide, with continuous improvements based on user feedback and technological advancements.
